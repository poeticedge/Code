Associate Installer Certification

Associate Installer Certification is your first step to becoming a certified Control4 Automation Programmer. This is an online course featuring four short classes that you'll be required to take before enrolling in the four-day Automation Programmer Certification course.

  • Four Short Online Classes
  • Can Be Completed in One Day
  • Required Before Taking Automated Programmer Certification

Automation Programmer Certification

Following your required pre-requisite Associate Installer Certification online course, you can enroll in our four-day Automation Programmer course at a Control4 Training center that works best for you.

  • Four-Day Classroom Training
  • Multiple Locations Available
  • Requires Associate Installer Certification

What to Expect

This class is hands-on and led by an expert Control4 instructor. You'll have your own learning station outfitted with the latest home automation products, and the course concludes with a certification exam. This course will provide the essential training needed to be successful in the field, so come prepared to work hard, be on time, and dedicated.

Pre-Requisites

You MUST take the Associate Installer Certification online course before enrolling in the Automation Programmer Certification class. Successful students often have a basic understanding of networking, audio-visual connections, and computer skills.
