Professional Certified Network Administrator

This online certification features six courses that include guided, hands-on learning with actual networking equipment. PCNA, Professional Certified Network Administrator, is the only AV and automation-centric networking certification that combines guided, hands-on learning with expertly-developed, award-winning curriculum that can be completed entirely from home. It focuses on the networking knowledge and experience that custom integrators need to deploy better, more robust networks—particularly networks that support AV and automation systems—which ultimately leads to a more effective team of technicians and happier customers, both of which are ultimately good for business.

  • Format: Online
  • Length: Approximately 20 hours
  • Prerequisites: None
