Ultra Black ALR

Precision-engineered for performance, Dragonfly's striking charcoal-grey Ultra Black ALR is an ideal choice for any professional space. It absorbs and rejects excess ambient light in a room, and eliminates shimmer and hotspots so every image features deep contrast and rich colors.

  • Ambient Light Rejecting material absorbs and rejects excess natural light
  • Premium material is optimized for 4K Ultra HD
  • Available in fixed, motorized, and recessed motorized

Ultra AcoustiWeave™

For auditoriums, houses of worship, or other corporate settings that need both gorgeous imagery and impeccable sound, use Dragonfly's Ultra AcoustiWeave. This finely-woven fiberglass material is acoustically transparent, so it transmits crystal-clear audio through the screen without damping the sound quality one bit.

  • Acoustically transparent material transmits sound through the screen while maintaining top-notch visual performance
  • Premium material is optimized for 4K Ultra HD
  • Available in fixed, motorized, and recessed motorized

Ultra White

For installations where you want bright whites and bold colors to pop, use Dragonfly's Ultra White to give high-definition video format an extra boost. This textureless material is perfect for showing off anything from a presentation to the big game.

  • Invigorates bright whites and provides optimum color contrast
  • Premium material is optimized for 4K Ultra HD
  • Available in fixed, motorized, and recessed motorized

High Contrast

This smoky grey 1080p screen not only improves overall color contrast, but adds a wider spectrum of subtler blacks and darker colors to high-res images. It's perfect for environments where natural light comes in from all sides, like bars, retail spaces, or conference rooms.

  • Prevents washed-out projection in well-lit rooms
  • Enhances image contrast and provides deep black saturation
  • Available in fixed, motorized, and recessed motorized

Matte White

Simple and elegant, this high-quality matte white material features a unique micro-diamond pattern; it amplifies whites and colors and provides a fantastic viewing experience every time.

  • High-quality 1080p delivers gorgeous color reproduction
  • Unique micro-diamond pattern reduces light ripples for better viewing
  • Available in fixed, motorized, and recessed motorized
