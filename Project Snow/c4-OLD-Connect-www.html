WHAT TO EXPECT

Control4 Connect enables many of the key features that make your Control4 system a truly smart experience. It also empowers your independent Control4 Professional with the ability to make sure your system is regularly updated and supports the latest and greatest features, integrations, and security updates.

Your enrollment in the Control4 Connect service makes the following features and functions possible*:

Software Development

Software improvements and security updates are part of living with technology and are essential for reliability and long-term satisfaction. Control4 Connect allows us to invest in long-term software development and makes it possible for your system to receive updates that improve user experience and add new features and integrations.

Control4 App (Mobile)

With Control4 Connect you can use the Control4 App for iOS and Android to personalize, view, control, and get notified about what is going on in your home or business. Whether at home or abroad, as long as you have an Internet connection, you can use your own tablet or mobile phone to be alerted of events and be able to respond. See “Remote System Access”, ”Mobile Notifications”, and “When>>Then Scene Personalization” for examples.

Remote System Access

Control your system with the Control4 App even when you’re not at home. Want to view and adjust your surveillance cameras? Need to lock a door that was accidentally left unlocked? Or maybe you’d like to set the temperature and the mood for when you arrive. All this is possible and more.

Mobile Notifications

Create your own personalized push notifications to alert you when people come and go, if a water leak is detected, or even if the garage door has been left open. Whether it’s urgent and needs immediate attention, or you simply want a status update, Control4 will keep you in the know by sending notifications to your tablet, mobile phone, or Apple Watch via the Control4 App.

Voice Control

Control4 Connect enables Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant integration so you can use your voice to control a specific device, a few devices simultaneously, or scenes across your whole home or business.

Intercom Anywhere

With Intercom Anywhere you can monitor and communicate with any room of your property, even the front door, right from your mobile device or Control4 Touchscreen. Whether you’re abroad or in the office, you can greet guests or delivery drivers via the Chime Video Doorbell—you can even allow them to enter. Do you have kids or employees that never answer the phone? See them and check in by intercomming into a Control4 Touchscreen from your phone or another touchscreen.

When>>Then Scene Personalization

Your Control4 professional orchestrates the devices in your home or business with advanced scene programming so everything works in harmony. With When>>Then you can add additional scenes and adjust them when seasons, moods, and schedules change. Adjust outdoor lights as sunset changes, add a new playlist to your “Cooking” scene, and reduce your “Dinner Time” lighting by 10 percent—with When>>Then the system is yours to fine-tune.

System Backup

Control4 Connect backs up your programming and preferences in order to restore your system if necessary.




*Some features are dependent on hardware and system configuration.
