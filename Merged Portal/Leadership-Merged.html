The SnapAV Team

Our leadership team has decades of experience in custom integration and distribution. They’ve helped build businesses and worked with integrators and customers on a personal level. Using this experience, they now lead a team of pros to deliver a smooth and profitable experience to our partners with advanced solutions for every job.

JOHN HEYMAN

Chief Executive Officer

John Heyman has worked in the technology industry for over 30 years. He has served as CEO of SnapAV as well as Radiant Systems, Inc., a publicly traded business until its $1.3 billion sale in 2011.

Throughout his tenure with SnapAV, John has led the company to tremendous growth with the introduction of industry-leading products, outstanding service, and a culture based on customer obsession. Most recently, he headed an industry-transforming merger between SnapAV and Control4 to help technology partners and professionals bring to life amazing technology experiences to homes and businesses.

John and his wife, Erin, live in Atlanta. He has three sons and enjoys golf, fly fishing, exercise, reading, and skiing. He is a graduate of the Harvard Business School and the University of Georgia, where he served on the Board of Advisors of the Terry College of Business and the Board of Visitors of the University.

MIKE CARLET

Finance

Mike has served as CFO at SnapAV and both COO and CFO of the automotive division of Sears Holdings. Before Sears, Mike spent over 15 years with Driven Brands, Inc., the parent company of Meineke Car Care Centers, Inc., Maaco Franchising, Inc., where he played a key role in establishing and implementing ongoing strategies that allowed Driven to become one of the largest providers of automotive service in the United States. He has also served as a member of the Executive Committee of the Board of Directors and Treasurer of the AutoCare Association.

Mike has been very involved with a number of nonprofit organizations and was the treasurer of the Ada Jenkins center, a facility dedicated to helping families break the cycle of poverty and create lasting solutions for economic stability.

Mike received his undergraduate degree in accounting from The Catholic University of America (where he was named “the Outstanding Accounting Student” for his class) and his MBA from Wake Forest University. He was recognized as one of Charlotte’s “40 under 40” in 2006. When not at work or with family, Mike relieves his stress as a drummer in a couple of local bands.

JEFF HINDMAN

Sales, Marketing, Support & Local

Before the merger of Control4 and SnapAV, Jeff joined SnapAV in 2016 as President of New Markets. Before that, Jeff spent 8 years with Radiant Systems and NCR (which acquired Radiant) in a variety of roles, most recently as General Manager of NCR’s Hospitality SMB division. Prior to Radiant, Jeff was a consultant with McKinsey & Company and a technology investment banker with JPMorgan.

Jeff received his MBA from Harvard Business School and a BSE in Mechanical Engineering from Duke University. He resides in Atlanta with his wife and two sons.

JEFF DUNGAN

Operations

Jeff majored in Computer and Electrical Engineering at Purdue University and graduated from Colorado State University with this degree after transferring to take a job at Hewlett Packard.

After holding senior positions in manufacturing, supply chain/logistics, and global program management at HP and Celestica, Jeff transitioned to BEA systems in the Bay Area (now a part of Oracle) to run IT for all G&A functions.

He spent the next fourteen years at Control4 in various leadership positions in Product Marketing, Business Development, and Operations, including demand planning, supply chain, logistics, and fulfillment. Upon the merger between Control4 and SnapAV, he was named Executive Vice President of Operations.

Jeff and his wife, Quyen, reside in Salt Lake City, Utah, and have a blended family with eight kids ranging from age five to 26 who keep them very busy—and, in his words, always tired!

WALLY WHINNA

Local Distribution

Wally is a 35-year veteran in the distribution business as the leader of Allnet, which was acquired by SnapAV in 2018, providing SnapAV with a brick-and-mortar presence for distribution in the Midwest.

Wally continues to lead local distribution efforts to bring brands, services, support, knowledge, and advice closer to our partners, where SnapAV now has the most local branches of any CE-focused distributor. His strategy is to expand SnapAV’s local footprint to service even more partners in their respective locations.

Wally and his wife of more than 40 years have two children and four grandchildren that they absolutely adore. They spend their summers in Chicago and their winters in Florida, enjoying the perfect weather with friends and family all year long.

JD ELLIS

Legal & Human Resources

DAVID MOORE

Head of Engineering & Technology
