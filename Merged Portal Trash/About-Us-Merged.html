Reinventing Custom Integration

Created out of respect and passion for the industry, SnapAV was founded by professional integrators like yourself who saw a better way to do things. We had been in the field, navigated tiny crawlspaces, and struggled with middlemen. We realized the industry needed a stronger advocate for your success, and since 2005, we’ve been just that.

Industry Pioneers

We're a manufacturer of innovative in-house brands and a distributor of cutting-edge third-party products, giving our Partners more products in one convenient location. Our in-house brands are built with an integrator-first approach and are easy to use and price-protected. And our third-party brands are some of the most popular in the industry, providing a comprehensive portfolio to give customers the exact solution they need.
