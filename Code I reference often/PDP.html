Araknis 420 Series PoE+ Network Switch

Araknis Networks 420 Series switches offer entry-level multi-gig functionality with flexible and reliable professional-grade hardware. Featuring advanced capabilities such as Layer 2 and Lite Layer 3 network management, networking professionals can better tailor the network experience to meet customer needs. Available with front- or rear-facing RJ-45 ports, each offering partial multi-gig capability, making these switches an excellent choice for any project, large or small.

The switches offer the same familiar user experience and interface you've come to expect from Araknis and include built-in OvrC support, providing easy and intuitive remote access to manage network settings and troubleshooting capabilities.

Additionally, these switches feature redesigned cooling fans that provide more efficient cooling while significantly reducing fan noise, even under load.

Configurations Available:

  • AN-420-R-44-POE (44 Port POE - Rear Facing Ports)
  • AN-420-F-48-POE (48 Port POE - Front Facing Ports

Fully Managed Layer 2 and Lite Layer 3 Management

This switch supports Lite Layer 3 and Layer 2 functionality for more complex network deployments. Layer 3 increases network efficiency by leveraging static, inter-VLAN routing to route traffic locally rather than relying entirely on the router.

2.5Gbps Multi-gig Throughput

With blazing fast 2.5Gbps available on certain ports, these switches are suited for high-speed applications like Audio/Video over IP, Binary MOIP, Wi-Fi 6, and ISP services up to 2.5Gbps.

Quad SFP+ Transceiver Ports for Fiber

Take your network to the next level with SFP+ 10G uplinking. These switches offer 4 SFP+ transceiver ports, allowing for connectivity via additional cables such as fiber for uplinking and data transfer over greater distances.
