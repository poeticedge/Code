Partner Rewards FAQs

  • How did I receive my level in Partner Rewards?

    You are automatically enrolled in the program once you reach the minimum spend threshold during the program year. Enrollment in the program begins at $10,000.

  • How long do I maintain my Partner Rewards level?

    By achieving your spend goal during the program year. The program year begins the first business day of Q3 and ends the last business day of Q2. Your reward level and benefits are locked in during the full duration of the program year.

  • Can I move up a level during the program?

    Yes, if you reach the next purchase level threshold, you will be moved up and begin receiving the benefits at that level. Please allow three business days for the level change to occur.

  • Can I move down a level during the program?

    No, you cannot move down from your current level until the next program cycle begins. The program year ends the last business day of Q2. Your next program year level will be determined by your previous program year spend.

  • Does my spend at Snap One partner Stores count towards my reward spend?

    Yes, all applicable Snap One purchases made at our Snap One Partner Stores count towards your reward spend.

  • Who is eligible to receive a rebate?

    Partners at the Premier level and above are eligible to receive quarterly rebates based on their quarterly spend on applicable purchases. There will be exclusive opportunities for partners in Reserve and Select to earn promotional rebates.

  • What are the terms of earning a rebate?

    Eligible partners must meet their quarterly spend goal to receive their quarterly rebate. Click here for additional information.

  • What is the period for earning a rebate?

    Rebates are earned and paid out on a quarterly basis.

  • Which products earn rebates?

    All Snap One products except for SunBrite, OvrC Pro, and 3rd party products are eligible to earn rebates.

  • How will I receive my rebate?

    Your rebate will be sent out in the form of a check on a quarterly basis.

  • Will my rebate count against my annual spend?

    No, your rebate will not count against your spend.

  • Who is NOT eligible for Free Shipping?

    Partners in Hawaii, Alaska, and outside of the U.S. do NOT qualify for free shipping.

  • How do I see my current spend and rewards level?

    You can view your current spend and rewards level details on your Snap One homepage dashboard, and under My Account ➤ Rewards Status. You will also receive ongoing emails detailing your spend.
