4The Install Handoff

You have already designed, ordered and received your Lutron products

Now, using the site survey guide from Step 2, begin grouping devices by install location, in gang order from left to right. Band all groups together and place them in front of the actual locations in the home as you walk through with the electrician.

Note: for dealers who have to know it all, Lutron has provided a wiring tool on their website with detailed installation information.

5Configuring The System

You don’t need computers to automate this lighting system!

Make sure your tabletop Picos and lamp modules are all in place, then begin system configuration. Claim the master repeater on your OvrC account, open the Lutron app, and select the Contractor Setup button. You’ll have the system up and running in no time.

You can make configuration changes either on-site or remotely via OvrC’s desktop or mobile app.
