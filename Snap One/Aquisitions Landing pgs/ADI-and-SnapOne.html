ADI and Snap One

Snap One is Now Part of ADI Global Distribution

We're excited to share that Snap One is now part of ADI Global Distribution, expanding our smart technology offerings and distribution capabilities across the residential and commercial markets. 

 

Together, we will offer a wide selection of both proprietary and third-party products through an omni-channel shopping experience with an extensive physical branch footprint and industry leading digital capabilities.

 

With increased product breadth, local availability, and broad industry expertise, we're confident that our combined business will meet your needs, exceed your expectations, and deliver even greater value to you.

FAQs

  • Who do I call if I have questions?

    If you have questions regarding the integration of ADI and Snap One not addressed here, please reach out to either team's customer support line.
    ADI | +1.888.684.9167 | adicsg@adiglobal.com
    Snap One | +1.866.424.4489 | sales@snapone.com

  • Do you expect ADI branches or Snap One Partner Stores to close due to this integration?

    Improving the customer experience and furthering our industry-leading store network are key priorities of any decisions for Snap One Partner Stores and ADI Branches.

    As we integrate the businesses, we will look for opportunities to enhance our footprint, expand our assortment, and improve the customer experience. Our site review is underway to finalize our strategy on delivering this enhanced experience with more options for our customers. This strategy may include consolidating store footprints in some markets, expanding our footprint in other markets, and enhancing our offering in all markets.

  • If I'm a customer of both Snap One and ADI today, do I need to do anything differently?

    You can continue to transact with Snap One and/or ADI as you did prior to the companies combining. In the future, we will launch enhancements aimed at delivering greater value and an improved overall experience.

  • I am an existing customer with ADI / Snap One, will I be required to get another / different account ID to purchase
    something in the other store (Snap One to ADI, or ADI to Snap One)?

    If you have existing accounts with Snap One or ADI, please continue to use your existing accounts and log-ins. If you are interested in transacting with Snap One or ADI and do not have an account, you will need to create one.

  • I am an existing customer with ADI / Snap One, do I need to "apply" to make purchases from the other company?

    Yes, all customers must have accounts with ADI and Snap One to transact with both companies. If you're interested in transacting with an entity that you don't do business with today, please reach out to your sales contact or branch team, they can assist you through the application process. You can also call 502.261.1793 or email Becomeacustomer@adiglobal.com.

  • How will websites, online portals and local stores be impacted? Will there be changes to where and how I purchase today?

    For now, there are no changes to the award-winning ecommerce portals or physical storefronts you use to transact with Snap One or ADI today.

    Over time, we aim to combine the best of ADI and Snap One’s digital and physical storefront experiences. We are building a company that is even better together and will deliver the industry’s best and most robust offering.

  • Will my sales contact person change?

    There are no immediate changes to sales contacts as a result of the combination of ADI and Snap One. If future changes are made, these will be communicated to you at the appropriate time.

  • Will my payment terms or credit limits be changed with ADI or Snap One now that the companies are combined?

    Initially, there will be no changes to credit limits or payment terms for each respective storefront.

    We will consider changes in the future as we combine the businesses and look to provide a streamlined and improved customer experience.

  • What happens if I'm on credit hold with one organization but not the other?

    If your business is on credit hold with ADI or Snap One, you will be subject to being placed on hold with the other. If both accounts are in good standing, the hold will be removed from both entities. Please reach out to customer support (ADI) or accounting (Snap One) if you have any questions or concerns with your account(s).

  • Will my loyalty / rewards benefits from ADI / Snap One be accessible across both entities? Will loyalty rewards programs be impacted in 2024?

    The existing Snap One and ADI loyalty programs will remain separate for the remainder of 2024, without any changes. We will evaluate opportunities to bring an improved, consolidated loyalty experience to customers after the end of this year.

  • Will Snap One / ADI freight shipping policies change immediately to match one another?

    No, freight shipping policies for ADI and Snap One will remain in place as they were prior to the combination of ADI and Snap One. We will evaluate changes in the future to provide you with a unified and enhanced customer experience.

  • Where do I get training for a product that I'm interested in from the other company that I'm not already transacting with?

    If you're interested in learning more about any of ADI or Snap One's products, please reach out to your sales contact and they can ensure you get more information as we roll out more products across both organizations.

  • Should I change the way I return a product?

    You should continue with the same process for returning a product to the ADI or Snap One location/channel in which it was purchased. If you have any further questions or need assistance, reach out to your ADI or Snap One customer service team.

  • Should I change where I call for tech support?

    There are no changes to either company’s customer support policies or procedures. Please continue to utilize technical and customer support as you did prior to the combination of ADI and Snap One.

  • Can I call Snap One for tech support on third-party product purchased at ADI?

    For products purchased at ADI, you will continue to follow the ADI process.

  • When will Snap One manufactured products be sold at ADI and vice versa?

    One of the benefits of the acquisition is the opportunity to expand product distribution across both storefronts. As of June 26th, we are pleased to bring select WattBox SKUs to the market through ADI. This is the first step of many to deliver on this benefit. We will communicate additional updates on further expansion on an ongoing basis.

  • Will Control4 products be available to non-Authorized products?

    No, Control4 products will continue to be sold only to Authorized Control4 Partners. The current Control4 authorization and certification processes remains in effect.
