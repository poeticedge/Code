WHAT TO EXPECT

Connect is a required service that must be paid annually through the Control4 app. Your Control4 Connect service makes the following features and functions possible*.

Access to Software Updates

Get access to software and security updates that help your system evolve and improve over time. These updates are crucial components of living with technology and are essential for long-term satisfaction, keeping your system reliable and secure.

System Control & Functionality

Control your system quickly and easily from your Control4 app, while enjoying access to the latest features and functionality as they become available and benefiting from the app's ongoing evolution and improvement.

Remote System Access

Access your system with the Control4 app even when you’re at a different location.

Mobile Notifications

Create your own personalized push notifications to your tablet, mobile phone, or Apple watch via the Control4 app. You will be alerted when people come and go, if a water leak is detected, or even if the garage door has been left open.

Voice Control

Use your voice to control a specific device, a few devices simultaneously, or scenes across your whole home or business. Control4 Connect easily enables Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant integration.

Intercom Anywhere

Monitor and communicate with any room of your property, even the front door, right from your mobile device or Control4 Touchscreen.

When>>Then Scene Personalization

Add additional scenes and adjust them when seasons, moods, and schedules change with the When >> Then feature. Your professional integrator will have already orchestrated the devices in your home or business with advanced scene programming so everything works in harmony.

System Backup

Feel confident knowing that Control4 backs up your programming and preferences to restore your system if necessary.

System Insights

Review your Control4 system's activities every month with a personalized system insights report. See useful information like uptime, downtime, if you are on the latest software and more.

System Education

Learn and troubleshoot from system education, accessible via the mobile app, allowing you to understand your system better and become a more confident user.

Apple Music†

Enjoy a wide range of benefits from Apple Music®, including Recommended For You, curated and collaborative ad-free playlists, and access to personal libraries. Additionally, Control4 audio to life, providing exquisite clarity, one-touch access, and intuitive multi-room controls. Easily access your Favorited media and Recently Played content, distribute and share across zones through the Sessions page, and take advantage of the ultra-fine EQ controls to fully personalize your sound experience. You can also manage playback with a simple touch on your Apple Watch® and explore a world of musical discoveries with intuitive ease.




*Some functions and features are dependent on hardware and system configuration. *†To use Apple Music, the following are required: A CORE Controller or Triad SA1, Control4 OS 3.4.2+, an active Connect plan or 4Sight subscription, and an Apple Music subscription (customers have the option to create a new Apple Music account during the setup process). *Some functions and features are dependent on hardware and system configuration.
