SunBrite Solis Full Sun Smart TV

SunBrite offers a TV for every outdoor space, making a smart, safe, and worry-free entertainment experience the industry standard.

  • 1500 nits of brightness from direct LED backlight
  • Anti-glare commercial screen
  • 4k60 resolution with 4k upscaling and HDR10
  • Built-in speakers, voice control functions
  • OvrC Pro-enabled Web OS TV

Episode Surroundscape 

Replaces the Episode Landscape Series for better performance, higher reliability, and easier installation.

  • IP67-rated satellite speakers, in 4”, 6”, and 8” sizes
  • Burial and hardscape subwoofers with high-excursion drivers
  • Quick-connect, solid aluminum stake, surface, or conduit mounts

Episode Radiance

This is a first-of-its-kind product line that combines outdoor audio and landscape lighting on a single two-conductor wire.

  • Modular system offers bollard speakers, light modules, subwoofer, MB1 audio and light processor, and mounts
  • Installation is a standard 70V approach, with the flexibility to scale the system and place audio and lighting anywhere 
  • Scalable system for any size space: add speakers, lights, and subs to fit your needs

Episode Hybrid Amps 

Replacing the Episode 70V/8-ohm IP-Enabled Amplifier, the Episode 600W and 1200W Hybrid Amplifiers provide class-leading power input.

  • Offers an updated configuration UI to streamline installer setup and education
  • Power high-performance 70V/8-ohm landscape systems (70V satellites with low-impedance subwoofers)
  • OvrC-enabled for remote management

Araknis

New offerings from Araknis include new 420 Series switches that feature multi-gig support, 320 Series access points, and essential supporting accessories like transceiver modules and in-ceiling mounting brackets.

  • 2.5 Gbps routers with built-in OvrC Pro Hub
  • Up to 10Gbps Layer 3 PoE++ switches that feature a new limited warranty of up to 5 years
  • Indoor/outdoor Wi-Fi 6 access point solutions – for a complete “from curb to client” multi-gig networking solution

Access Networks

Access Networks switches are designed for tough network environments requiring peak performance and capabilities.

  • Advanced Layer 3 functionality
  • Enterprise-grade components
  • Sophisticated network traffic management
  • Designed for optimal performance where milliseconds of latency matter 
  • OvrC Enabled Unleashed Wi-Fi 6 Access Points
  • 12, 24, and 48-port switches now available on the Snap One Partner Portal

OvrC 

Built for professionals, OvrC is celebrating ten years as the ultimate configuration, deployment, diagnostic and troubleshooting tool. Check out the latest updates:

  • OvrC configuration of Access Networks APs and management in OvrC Connect
  • Completely reimagined Wi-Fi Management process
  • Enhanced Device Claiming process

WattBox 

WattBox 820 Series 20-amp PDUs come in 2, 8, and 12-outlet models and feature individual outlet metering, surge protection, and power conditioning to reduce service calls and protect equipment.

  • Engineered for high-power jobs
  • Customization through OvrC
  • Self-healing auto reboot
  • “Zero down time" battery backup
