Audio Outside

Whether you’re working with a restaurant on their patio dining area, in a homeowner’s backyard, or anywhere in between, Snap One has the right products for any outdoor install.

The Perfect Outdoor Experience

Outdoor audio is most effective when it provides full, robust sound while blending into the environment. Placement and spacing are crucial to an effective installation, so for a small deck or patio, we recommend mounting left and right speakers about 10 feet apart. For larger spaces, choose a set of satellite speakers - these look like landscape lighting fixtures, and provide broad yet targeted coverage without disturbing the neighbors.

Bring The Bass

Install a hardscape subwoofer that blends with an outdoor aesthetic and delivers deep, immersive, low-frequency sound. Hide the device without losing quality bass by installing a burial sub.

How Many Outdoor Speakers Do I Need?

A single pair will cover a small deck or patio of around 100-200 square feet. To maintain consistent sound, add one speaker per 10-15 feet beyond the initial pair.

How To Power Your Outdoor Speakers

You can use an amp or receiver—whichever your install needs. The important thing is to choose good speaker wire that will hold up for outdoor runs, through-wall runs, and underground runs, which is why we stock direct burial and plenum speaker wire from Wirepath!
