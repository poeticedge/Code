Every TV Needs a Soundbar

As TVs get thinner, internal speakers get smaller, with drivers the size of headphones that produce thin sound with zero bass. A good soundbar solves this. At Episode, we build ours with premium components at an easy-to-sell price, delivering theater-like sound on any budget.
