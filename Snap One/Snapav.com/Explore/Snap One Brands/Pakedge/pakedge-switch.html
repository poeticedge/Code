Professional-Grade Pakedge MS Series Switches

Engineered for Performance

Choose the MS Series for large audio and video streaming projects and multi-rack jobs where maximum bandwidth is a must, such as large homes or commercial settings like sports bars, retail spaces, and more.

OvrC-Enabled

Pros around the world trust OvrC, and now you can harness the power of remote management right out of the box with no additional hardware required. With OvrC, you can manage PoE power and consumption per port, reboot ports, update firmware, and more – all 100% remotely via the OvrC desktop or mobile app.

Dealer-Inspired Versatility

With five switches in 12-, 24-, and 44-port models, the MS Series offers a range of options to fit the needs of every project. MS Layer 3 Switches offer 1Gbps Ethernet, PoE/PoE+, and optional 10Gbps SFP+ fiber uplinks, making them perfect for high-speed switch-to-switch connections and large media or video over IP deployments.

Advanced Residential & Commercial Functionality

MS Switches support Layer 2 functionality including VLANs, IGMP, and spanning tree, in addition to professional AV protocols like Dante and Q-LAN. For more advanced jobs, these switches also provide Layer 3 functionality.

Unmanaged Switches

The Pakedge SE Series gives you a choice of unmanaged switches with a range of port, PoE, and localized power options to help you meet the demands of any install.
