Take Control of the Network with OvrC

Together, Pakedge and OvrC help you remotely set up and manage your customer’s network from an easy to use mobile or desktop app. OvrC allows you to reboot unresponsive devices, manage PoE power and consumption, update firmware, and modify configurations – all 100% remotely.

With built-in OvrC Pro, keeping your customers’ networks up and running is easier than ever before. OvrC Pro extends your visibility and access to the entire network of IP devices, including non-SnapAV products, all within the OvrC platform.

Switches

OvrC gives you detailed information and control for each individual port for unmatched troubleshooting. Get switch connection status, power consumption, and more from your office or couch at any time of day.

Access Points

Setting up networks with multiple access points is a breeze with OvrC and Wi-Fi Management. The ability to discover and configure all connected APs from a single dashboard and view channel and power outputs to ensure optimal coverage will eliminate hours of manual setup time while helping you deliver proactive customer service.
