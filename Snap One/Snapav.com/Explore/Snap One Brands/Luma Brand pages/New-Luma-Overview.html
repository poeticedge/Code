The Power of AI Technology

Reduce false notifications and have your clients respond more accurately to them with the latest AI technology including advanced human, vehicle, and non-motor vehicle detection, as well as AI-filtered events. With AI-filtered events, your clients can quickly be notified, share, and view key moments their system finds throughout the day.

Human Detection

View foot traffic, delivery people, or anyone else as they enter a Luma camera’s view. Through the Luma View app, clients can receive notifications and view events for enhanced peace of mind, even when they’re away.

Vehicle Detection

Luma’s advanced AI can detect vehicles, which can then send a notification, record a snapshot, create an event-based recording or even trigger a series of events when using a control system (ex. When a car enters the driveway, turn on pathway lighting).

Non-Motor Vehicle Detection

Give your clients more accurate alerts with activity from non-motor vehicles like bikes or scooters.

Keeps Client’s Privacy Safe

Maintaining your client’s privacy and safety is essential for your business. Luma is built with privacy settings and features so the client can be assured that they are in control of their information.

After the installation is complete, a customer handoff from the installer to the client happens giving them exclusive control and access to live videos and passwords. After this, only the client can view live video and can choose to give you access for troubleshooting purposes. Additionally, cameras never have their microphones enabled by default and can be turned on via Luma View as needed – or disabled altogether via OvrC.

Luma Cameras

The Luma family of IP cameras is available in Bullet, Turret, Dome, and PTZ. Ranging from 2MP to 8MP resolution, fixed and varifocal lens options are also available. With a robust line of solutions, you can choose from cameras that include 24/7 color with Color at Night technology, improved clarity in low-light environments with Starlight, or IR imagery for dim settings. Luma cameras are fully NDAA-compliant and have you covered for any job that requires it.
