Individually Controlled & Metered Outlets

Control each outlet independently for advanced troubleshooting and the ability to reboot single devices without disrupting the entire system. Schedule precise outlet power cycles and remote system start-up or create a customized sequence via OvrC to ensure your client’s system gets back up and running in no time.

Metered Outlets

Individual outlet metering and entire device metering gives you unmatched voltage insight, right down to the power consumed by each outlet. Now you can easily solve for those frustrating situations where a client has unknowingly unplugged a device before making a service call.

Control Integration

IP WattBox units are compatible with Control4, Crestron, Elan, RTI, URC, and a next-generation Telnet API that lets you create unique solutions to deploy and monitor WattBox products across thousands of sites. Features are dependent upon the control system but may include discreet on and off control, safe voltage status, and power metering per outlet.
