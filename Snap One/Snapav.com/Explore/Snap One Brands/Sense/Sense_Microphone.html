This handheld microphone transmitter is a great companion for our wireless receiver. Its superior audio pickup ensures you get the sound you want, and its advanced build quality promises excellence for a long time to come. It’s easy to set up, too, so it’s perfect for training rooms, auditoriums, and stage performances.

New Regulations - Due to recent regulations from the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) and the repurposing of the 600MHz band of the UHF spectrum, SnapAV is required to transition to an all-new Sense product line which will operate in the 500Mhz band. All Sense products currently operating in the 600Mhz spectrum band will be discontinued as of October 13, 2018, and will not be compatible with devices operating in the 500Mhz spectrum band. Starting July 13, 2020, FCC regulations will prohibit use of the 600MHz band of the UHF spectrum by consumer devices. Learn more about affected SKUs >>
