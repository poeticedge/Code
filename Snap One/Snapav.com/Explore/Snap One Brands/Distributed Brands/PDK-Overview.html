PDK

Mobile-First Access Control

PDK™ makes wireless access management easier than ever. With wireless locks, cloud-based management, and easy credentialing, the system enhances security and peace of mind at commercial locations, high-end residences, gated communities and beyond. A variety of credentialing options means each business or owner can choose to utilize check-ins relying on Bluetooth, mobile push buttons, wearable credentials or high-security fobs or keycards.

The mobile-focused platform allows users to track meeting schedules and employee attendance, set schedules for timed access, and much more. Their cloud-based design also simplifies maintenance and upkeep by limiting onsite hardware and providing automatic updates and enhancements as they are released.
