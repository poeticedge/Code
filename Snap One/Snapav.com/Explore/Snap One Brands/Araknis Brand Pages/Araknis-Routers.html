Multi-Gig VPN Routers with OvrC Pro

In the age of abundant Internet speeds, the Araknis® line can support your customer’s network no matter how big or small while also offering versatile solutions to fit any environment. Araknis routers provide a reliable foundation for the entire network, supporting up to multi-gigabit 2.5Gbps throughput, secure remote VPN connections, and OvrC for faster setup and robust remote management.

More than “Just” a Router

All Araknis products are compatible with OvrC to help streamline your installs and support your customers. Araknis routers also include OvrC Pro, which extends your visibility and access to the entire network of IP devices, including non-Snap One products. Plus, OvrC provides a powerful suite of troubleshooting tools including enhanced system health and diagnostic data that make remote support easy and eliminate unnecessary truck rolls.

Versatile Versions

Whether you’re installing in a rack, Versabox, or structured wiring can, Araknis has a router that fits the job. Choose between wireless, wired, 1U rack-mountable, or compact form factors.
