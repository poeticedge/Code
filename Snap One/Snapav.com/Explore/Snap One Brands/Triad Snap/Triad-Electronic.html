SA1 Streaming Amplifier

Support a single 2-channel audio zone within a Control4 system with 400w of audiophile-grade power. Enjoy high-resolution, lossless streaming from popular platforms like TIDAL, Pandora, Spotify, SiriusXM, and more with Ryff built-in. Enhanced DSP controls tailor sound to any space. HDMI ARC/eARC supports audio formats such as Dolby Atmos and DTS. Two IR outputs allow for local source control. Seamlessly enjoy perfectly synced music around the home with network audio synchronization.

Power Amplifiers

Triad Power Amplifiers have been redesigned for improved performance and reliability. Bridgeable Outputs provide 25% higher output with lower distortion. Global inputs and outputs allow flexibility while Digital Class-D architecture provides high-resolution audio even at high volumes. Use Triad Power Amplifiers with other Triad speakers, audio matrixes and Control4 CORE controllers to deliver a world-class, high-resolution audio experience.

Audio Matrix Switches

Triad AMS Audio Matrix Switches are designed to route and deliver high-resolution audio throughout the home. Connect sources with stereo RCA, digital coax, or digital optical and use the stereo RCA outputs to deliver audio to power amplifiers or AVRs. Provide two levels of 6-band parametric EQ to dial in precise sound in any space. Additionally, the custom-built Triad DSP engine and the improved 2.1 zone grouping feature allows for subwoofer implementation that delivers full range sound, subtle details, and exceptionally clear audio. An added input and output audio delay make for fast and easy audio/video synchronization.

RackAmp

Triad’s RackAmps are designed to pair with Triad subwoofers exclusively to produce high impact, explosive bass with films as well as nuanced, detailed, and tuneful bass with music. Provides clean, efficient power thanks to Class D amplifier technology. Easy to set up/control with compact and efficient 1RU sizing.
