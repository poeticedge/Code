Empower Your Customers

Activate the OvrC Connect app to simplify your customers' lives. This allows them to perform initial troubleshooting. Customize buttons, icons, reset/reboot functions, and include your dealership logo and contact details for advanced troubleshooting support.

Convenient

With the touch of a button, customers can fix small problems on their own schedule without needing to know system complexities.

Customizable

Create and update remote commands for your customers in OvrC Connect based on their individual needs. Through the app, they'll be able to reset any OvrC-enabled devices, plus devices connected to individual WattBox outlets or Araknis PoE ports. Customers can even reset multiple devices with a single touch, through a sequence of resets pre-programmed with OvrC Connect macros.

Learn more about OvrC Connect macros >>

Network Controls

In addition to device resets and reboots, the OvrC Connect app provides customers with controls to manage Wi-Fi access, and options to filter content and provide stricter network security.

Select the level of network security and content filtering desired to secure the network. Customers can choose to enable basic SafeSearch and malware protections, or stricter levels of security for content, file sharing, downloads, and online communication.

Control access to the internet and local network devices with Wi-Fi controls. OvrC Connect admins can create schedules that enable and restrict devices from wirelessly connecting to the network.

Help Clients Reach You

Add your company logo and contact information to OvrC connect, so customers can email or call you directly from the app.

Invite Multiple Users

The OvrC Connect app offers powerful controls and commands that can be used by multiple users. Your client has the freedom to specify who can access the account. Each new user will receive a personalized email invitation to download the app and set up their own credentials.

View Command History

OvrC Connect maintains a comprehensive log of all user reset commands, ensuring you can access information to troubleshoot devices effectively.
