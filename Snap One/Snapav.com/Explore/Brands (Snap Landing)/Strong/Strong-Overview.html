Mounts

The Razor™ and VersaMount™ lines deliver the industry’s lowest profiles to match today's thin TV, while our Carbon Series offers the strength and price point needed for the everyday install. Plus, our engineers have included installer-centric features like post-install leveling, easy adjusting, magnetic pull cords on our locking mechanisms for easy cord management, and UL listing for added reassurance. Strong™ mounts are available in fixed, tilt, motion, ceiling, and projector mounts.
