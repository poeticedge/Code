Floor Standing

The combination of robust form and versatility make our floor standing racks a go-to choice. Available in five different designs with pre-built and knock-down configurations, there's a Strong floor standing style to match your next project.

Compare Racks

Signature Series

Perfect for the job that requires the best, this pre-assembled series features sturdy, fully welded frames for heavy equipment, adjustable rack rails, and flat or curved smoked glass doors. Ample depth and width allow for larger gear and easy wire management.

Shop Signature

Custom Series

If you're one of those guys who prides himself on dressing out a rack to the nines, then Custom Series is for you. Our Z-Lock rail design and a skirted caster base come standard for faster assembly and servicing. Models are available in a variety of sizes and depths with options like smoked glass doors, side panels, cooling, and wire management..

Shop Custom

FS Series

Pre-built for fast installation, this series includes most of what you need right out the box like doors, side panels, casters, cooling fans, and an assortment of shelves and vents. Never before have you gotten so much value in a single click.

Shop FS

Contractor Series

Our most economical full AV rack includes side panels and fine floor casters, giving you all the basics you need at a great value. Ships in a knock-down configuration for easy transportation.

Shop Contractor

Evolve™ Series

Meet the world's first hybrid rack. Part rack and part shelving system, this pre-assembled rack system changes the game in performance and value. It installs in seconds, combining the ability to directly rack mount gear with the speed and simplicity of included adjustable shelves. Plus the height of the rack itself is adjustable for the perfect fit.
