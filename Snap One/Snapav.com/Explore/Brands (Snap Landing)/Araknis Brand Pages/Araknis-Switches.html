A Switch for Every Job

Araknis offers an extensive selection of enterprise-grade network switches to support all your network needs. From PoE capabilities, port counts and placements, and up to Layer 3 functionality and 10Gbps throughput, you can deliver reliable connectivity in any installation.

Know The Network

Araknis switches work with OvrC to extend configuration and management to the cloud, for faster deployments and remote troubleshooting without a truck roll. You can cycle ports, view port details, and more with OvrC.

A Form Factor to Fit Your Install

Araknis switches are available in a variety of form factors to suit nearly any project. With front and rear port options that feature LEDs front and back, rack mount options, and even wall-mount or structured wiring can solutions you can bring an enhanced networking solution to any space. Plus, you can choose from as few as 5 ports or as many as 48 ports (with accessories) depending on what you need for the job.
