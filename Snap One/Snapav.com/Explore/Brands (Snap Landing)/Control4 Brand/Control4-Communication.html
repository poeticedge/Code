Enhanced Communication and Security

Control4 Smart Home OS 3 gives customers secure visibility into their entire home. Customers can view security cameras, control door locks and lights, speak to visitors at the front door, and receive alerts from anywhere in the world for total peace of mind.

Chime Video Doorbell

Chime is designed for the Control4 Smart Home and offers greater security and better interoperability than other video doorbells. Customers can remotely unlock their door and greet guests with lights, music, and more from the Control4 App.
