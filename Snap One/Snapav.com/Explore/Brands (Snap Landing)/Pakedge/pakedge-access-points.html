Faster Speeds For Even More Devices

Pakedge WA Series Access Points are engineered to bring high-speeds and accelerated processing power to client-dense smart homes and businesses. Pakedge Access Points with Wave 2 technology and beamforming deliver fast speeds simultaneously to multiple high-speed devices and 4K/HD real-time applications with smooth roaming to every corner of a project.

Fast Roaming

Pakedge Access Points offer fast roaming to ensure wireless devices are always connected to the strongest connection, eliminating latency and guaranteeing optimal performance.

Sleek Design

WA Series APs feature an aesthetically designed in-ceiling flush-mount design that’s sure to delight your customers and can be easily hidden out of sight without compromising coverage. Or, choose an on-ceiling model to best suit your customer’s needs.

Outdoor Coverage

The WA-2200-O outdoor model extends exceptional performance to the outdoors while blending into your customer’s outdoor oasis. It features a dust and water-resistant IP55 rated design to withstand the harshest environments, plus flexible pole or wall mounting options for discreet installs.

Versatile & Easy Install

All WA Series Access Points include easy-to-use mounting templates and accessories to significantly reduce installation time using an easy 3-step process.

OvrC: From Setup to Troubleshooting

OvrC remote management enables fast setup, and Wi-Fi Management gives you the ability to push settings to one or multiple APs in a single click, helping you set up and configure your client’s networks in just minutes. Plus, important settings including channel, power levels, and roaming can be configured right from the OvrC web and mobile app. Learn more.
