We created our Partner Rewards Program for one simple reason – to thank you for your business. To show our appreciation for your loyalty, we’re helping you save on the products you buy most and offering exclusive perks to make doing business with us easier and more profitable than ever before.

Each year, we reevaluate the program based on dealer feedback and make adjustments accordingly. You told us that a full year of locked-in, upfront discounted pricing was the most important to your business. To continue delivering on that promise and the other great benefits of Partner Rewards, we needed to adjust the rebate portion of the program. Therefore, effective Q2 2019, all rebates will be paid based on quarterly spend.

The charts below detail the program's annual benefits, as well as the new quarterly rebate structure. Premier level dealers and above must meet their quarterly goal in order to receive their maximum rebate.

Third-Party Product Update

In order to further strengthen the benefits of Partner Rewards, effective Q2 2019, all existing third-party brands sold at both SnapAV and local branches will count toward your Partner Rewards spend. The following brands are included: Altronix, Arlington, Atlona, BenQ, Clare, ClearOne, Cool Components, FSR, LSDI, Lutron, MetaGeek, Nest, Pioneer, Planet Waves, Platinum Tools, SureCall, and URC. We hope this change makes it easier than ever to maximize your savings and rebate.

How do I join the Partner Rewards program?

No signup is required. You are automatically enrolled in the program at the Reserve level once you spend $10,000 during the program year on SnapAV-eligible products.
