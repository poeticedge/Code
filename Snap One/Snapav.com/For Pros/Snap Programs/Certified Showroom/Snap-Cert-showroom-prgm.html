Control4 Certified Showroom Program

Information and Resources

We know that Control4 Certified Showroom Professionals work on some of the most beautiful projects in the world – and we want to highlight your work! As you plan your upcoming projects, remember to slate in professional photography and video as part of your project wrap-up. Send that project photography and video to us so we can feature you on our site(s). You can submit project photography and video at  certifiedshowrooms@snapone.com.

Certified Showroom Spotlight

The Certified Showroom Gallery pages showcase the beauty and functionality of Control4® Certified Showrooms. Each Gallery page also populates the showroom’s Control4 dealer microsite, helping prospects researching online understand what each showroom offers.

You can have your own gallery page to market your business, too! Email certifiedshowrooms@snapone.com with at least six (6) high-res/professional photos, product/demo descriptions of those photos, any design-build professionals you want to be credited, and a quote, and we’ll add you to the Showroom Gallery. 

Certified Showroom Recertification

Certified Showroom Dealers must recertify at the beginning of every year to maintain their status in the program. This checklist will provide you with the standards that will be used during recertification for each CSD, so we suggest you familiarize yourself with this checklist now to ensure a smooth recertification process each year.
