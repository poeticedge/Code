Connect


POWERING THE POSSIBLE

Control4® Connect is the required software service to enable essential features that keep customers’ systems secure and up to date, give convenient access and insights, and ensure systems can connect with new devices on the market. It also provides features of a truly smart living experience, such as voice control, remote access, on-the-go visibility, and personalization of mobile notifications.

Control4 Connect has been developed for over a year in collaboration with our Partners, based on feedback from both customers and integrators. Connect is currently available and required only within the United States.

A Truly Smart Experience

Control4 Connect enables the following features:*

Control4 Connect provides access to software and security updates that help the system installed today evolve and improve over time allowing for new features and integrations – keeping it reliable and secure.

Control4 Connect enables Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant integration so customers can use their voice to control a specific device, a few devices simultaneously, or scenes across their whole home or business.

Customers can control their systems quickly and easily from the Control4 app while enjoying access to the latest features and functionality as they become available and benefiting from the app's ongoing evolution and improvement.

Customers can monitor and communicate with any room of their property, even the front door, right from their mobile device or Control4 Touchscreen. Whether customers are abroad or in the office, they can greet guests or delivery drivers via the Chime Video Doorbell—they can even allow visitors to enter.

Customers can control their systems with the Control4 app even when they are not at home. They can view and adjust surveillance cameras, lock a door that was accidentally left unlocked, or even set the temperature and mood for when they arrive.

Customers can add additional scenes and adjust them when seasons, moods, and schedules change. Customers have the ability to adjust outdoor lights as sunset changes, add a new playlist to their “Cooking” scene, and reduce their “Dinner Time” lighting by 10 percent—with When>>Then, the system is theirs to fine-tune.

Control4 Connect will keep customers in the know by sending notifications to their tablet, mobile phone, or Apple watch via the Control4 app. They can create personalized notifications to alert them when people come and go, if a water leak is detected, or even if the garage door has been left open.

Customers receive visibility into their Control4 system’s activities every month with a system insights report. They can review useful information like uptime, downtime, if they are on the latest software, and more. These insights will guide them as they consider personalization and scene changes.

Customers can learn and troubleshoot from system education, accessible via the mobile app, allowing them to understand their systems better and become more confident users.

Control4 Connect backs up user programming and preferences in order to restore a customer’s system if necessary.

How It Works

1. Required Service

As of April 23, 2024, all new systems installed in the United States will require Control4 Connect. We recommend you include Control4 Connect in your proposals as soon as possible so your customers are aware that payment for the service will be due beginning in April.

4. Recurring Revenue

Control4 will manage customer payments and compensate you monthly through ACH transfers for your share of the recurring revenue, as long as you remain the Dealer of Record for your customers with active Control4 Connect. This process removes the hassle of you having to operationalize payment collection and customer communications.

2. OvrC Registration and Handoff

Use OvrC to register the system before beginning configuration, as well as when handing off the system to your customer. This requires you to do a one-time mapping of your Control4 and OvrC accounts. After system hand off, your customer will receive an introductory email from us with instructions for how to download the Control4 app.

Note: As of April 23, 2024, all new Control4 systems must be registered & handed off using OvrC. Controller registration and account hand-off through all other methods will no longer be available. Click here for details.

5. Improved Experiences

Post-install, your customers will enjoy an improved experience owing to all the benefits Connect offers. You will also receive a monthly System Insights Report which gives you visibility into your customers’ plan selections and system status at a glance.

3. It’s all in the App

Within the app, your customer can set up their Control4 account and complete payment for Connect. The annual price is based upon the controller installed.

How To Get Started

  1. Learn more about Control4 Connect by reviewing the resources found below. Reach out to your Sales Rep with any questions.
  2. Provide your W9 and ACH information so you are qualified to begin receiving recurring revenue payments.
  3. Include the required Control4 Connect on your proposals and begin to enjoy recurring revenue. Connect will be required on all new systems beginning April 2024. Pricing varies per job based on the controller(s) installed.

What does this mean for 4Sight?

4Sight continues to be available for customers with systems installed prior to April 23, 2024. For any systems installed after April 23, 2024, Connect is the required software service that replaces 4Sight. This means:

  • Customers with systems installed prior to April 23, 2024, can purchase and renew 4Sight to enjoy the feature set it delivers
  • Partners will continue to receive a product credit for all annual sales and renewals of 4Sight
  • Snap One will invest in developing the capabilities and feature set of Connect over time, while 4Sight will remain as it is today
