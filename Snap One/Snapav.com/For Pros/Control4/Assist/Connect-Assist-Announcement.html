Assist & Assist Premium


ASSISTANCE FROM A DISTANCE

Control4® Assist and Control4® Assist Premium are optional next-level manufacturer support plans that complement the services provided by you. These services have been developed for over a year in collaboration with our Partners, based on feedback from both customers and integrators.

Only Assist Authorized Partners may sell Assist. You become authorized to sell Assist by taking the Control4 Assist Authorization course and agreeing to the Service Level expectations. Assist and Assist Premium support plans are only available in the United States on systems that have the Control4 Connect service enabled

With these plans, Assist Authorized Partners are able to offer an elevated customer experience, improve your team’s quality of life, and earn recurring revenue without needing to scale up your service department.

How It Works

1. Assist Authorized

Only Assist Authorized Partners may sell Assist. You become authorized to sell Assist by meeting certain qualifications, taking the Control4 Assist Authorization course, and agreeing to the Service Level expectations. If you’re interested in applying, you should reach out to your Sales Representative.

4. We Take the Calls, You Take the Credit

Your customers call us when they have problems instead of you, allowing your technicians and programmers to focus on revenue-generating work such as acquiring new clients or installing new projects.

2. Connect Customers Only

You may sell these remote support plans to new Control4 customers who have Connect enabled. Connect is required on all new Control4 systems in the United States beginning April 23, 2024.

5. Earn Recurring Revenue

Control4 will manage customer payment processing and compensate you monthly through ACH transfers for your share of the recurring revenue, as long as you remain the Dealer of Record for your customers with active Control4 Connect. This process removes the hassle of you having to operationalize payment collection and customer communications.

3. It’s All in the App

Your customer chooses the plan that best fits their needs and pays directly through the Control4 App.
