SEIZE THE DAY WITH DAYLIGHT

Daylight automatically adjusts the brightness and color temperature of fully tunable white (CCT) LEDs to mimic the natural light cycle of the sun, for better circadian health. Daylight matches the sunrise with warm, dim lighting. As the sun climbs in the sky, the lights gradually brighten and cool. Then, as the sun lowers back down, the lights will begin to warm in temperature and dim in brightness.

Partners can configure the Daylight settings after adding the Daylight Agent to a project equipped with CCT LEDâ€™s. End-users can use Control4 apps or Touchscreens to make adjustments to fit their personal routines by shifting the schedule earlier or later, and by changing how bright, dim, warm or cool the lighting gets.
