5 GREAT NEW FEATURES FOR THE HALO
& HALO TOUCH REMOTE

Halo Firmware 1.7 delivers powerful new features to enhance your customer’s experience and ensure their remote is always connected for their enjoyment, including:

  • Faster, improved reconnects
