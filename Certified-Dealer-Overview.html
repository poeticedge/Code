Do You Have What It Takes?

The Certified Showroom program isn’t for everyone. It requires an investment on your part—in time and money. Read through the requirements to see if the program makes sense for you.

Requirements:

  • Control4 must be the preferred and primary automation solution in your showroom
  • The showroom must boldly convey and merchandise the Control4 Experience
  • The showroom must be in a non-residential, dedicated professional location and be approved by an ASM and Regional Director. It cannot be in an occupied residence or a builder/developer showroom.
  • Solutions in the showroom must be automated into a complete lifestyle demonstration experience full of 1-touch magic delivering previously unimagined instant convenience and ambiance
  • Exterior and interior presentation, are important elements of the showroom experience and must reflect the elegance and contemporary feel that the Control4 brand represents
  • Your sales team must demonstrate competency in selling the Control4 Experience (to be evaluated by Control4 ASM)
  • You must be willing to commit to growth and work with your ASM to build an agreed-upon business plan
  • Solutions in the showroom must be automated into a complete lifestyle demonstration experience full of 1-touch magic delivering previously unimagined instant convenience and ambiance
