Partner Rewards FAQs

  • How did I receive my level in Partner Rewards?

    No signup was required. You were automatically enrolled in the program, and your current reward level was calculated based on your spend from July 2017 – June 2018.

  • How long do I maintain my Partner Rewards level?

    The program is annual. Your reward level is locked in until the program year ends on June 28, 2019, or your spend qualifies you to move up a rewards level.

  • Can I move up a level during the program?

    Yes, if you reach the next purchase level threshold, you will be moved up and begin receiving the benefits at that level. Please allow three business days for the level change to occur.

  • Can I move down a level during the program?

    No, you cannot move down from your current level until the next program cycle begins. This program cycle ends June 28, 2019. At this date, your level will be determined by your previous year’s spend.

  • Who is eligible to receive a rebate?

    Dealers at the Premier level and above will earn rebates on all eligible spend. There will be exclusive opportunities for dealers in Reserve and Select to earn promotional rebates.

  • What is the period for earning a rebate?

    Rebates are earned and paid out on a quarterly basis.

  • Which products earn rebates?

    All SnapAV products except for SunBrite, One-Stop and Affiliate Products are eligible to earn rebates

  • How will I receive my rebate?

    Your rebate will be sent out in the form of a check OR on a quarterly basis.

  • Will my rebate count against my annual spend?

    No, it will not.

  • Who is NOT eligible for Free Shipping?

    Dealers in Hawaii, Alaska, and outside of the U.S. do NOT qualify for free shipping.

  • How do I see my current spend and rewards level?

    You can view your current spend, rewards level, and benefits on your SnapAV homepage dashboard, or under My Account > Rewards Status. You will also receive a monthly email detailing your spend.

    Click here for full program terms and conditions or Email us at partnerrewards@snapav.com.
