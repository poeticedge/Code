Snap University FAQs

Need some help as you explore Snap University? Review some of the most frequently asked questions, troubleshooting tips, and best practices below. If you need additional support, send an email to training@snapav.com.

    Snap University Setup

  • Why can't [this employee] see the "Launch" button for Snap University?

    Snap University draws its users from our website’s list of registered users. Every user needs a unique login (email and password) so we can accurately track which lessons they have completed. If your employee doesn’t have a unique login to our website, they will be unable to access Snap University.

    The easiest way for you to get your employees enrolles is to compile a list with the following:

    First Name

    Last Name

    Email Address

    Send that information via email to sales@snapav.com. We'll add them to the database so they can start taking courses. Please note: processing can take 1-2 business days.

  • Logging In

    Snap University is accessed by logging into SnapAV.com. Click on the Training tab, then click the Launch button to open the LMS system.

  • Best Practices

    To have the best experience with Snap University, we recommend you follow the procedures below.

    • Multiple users should not share a single login.
    • Use Google Chrome.
    • Use a desktop computer rather than a mobile device.
    • Keep your browser current.
    • Exit lessons using the Exit Course button within the lesson.
    • When exiting a lesson, allow the system to return to the course info page before closing the window.
    • Some Snap University may require that you have Adobe Flash. Ensure Flash is enabled in your browser, and keep your Flash up to date.

  • Enrolling in and Launching a Course

    From the Training tab, click on a category. Snap University shows you all the courses available under that category. Click on a course. Snap University shows you the course details.

    • If the course is open enrollment, click Enroll or Enroll & Launch.
    • If the course is restricted, information boxes tell you what you must do to enroll.
    • When you open the Training tab, your current course enrollments appear on the right side of the page. Click on any of these course titles to go to that course’s page. Click Launch to start the course from your last position, or the Go button next to any eligible lesson to start that lesson from the beginning.

  • Changing Your Password

    Your password cannot be changed from within Snap University. Instead, change it on the SnapAV website by navigating to the My Account menu.

  • Checking for Current Enrollments

    Users can see they have been enrolled in courses under My Current Enrollmentin the My Info Section of the LMS

    • Course & Lesson Issues

  • Single Sign On (SSO) Error Message

    The Single sign on error appears if a user as not been added to Snap University. New users must be added by contacting Customer Service at sales@snapav.com with the first and last name and email address of the user requesting access.


    Note: This error will also appear if an account has been disabled.

  • I Can't Log in to Snap University

    You do not log in to Snap University from its homepage. Instead, you must log in at SnapAV.com. Once you are logged in, click the Training tab and then the Launch button to open the LMS system.

  • When I launch a lesson, I don’t see any visuals.

    You might not be able to view the content within the lesson if the lesson requires Flash. If you have Flash installed, you may need to adjust your Flash settings. See your browser’s FAQ for details.

  • My lesson is stuck or has frozen.

    In most cases, the lesson simply needs to be reset by an administrator. Please contact your administrator for assistance.

  • I see my completed course under my current enrollments.

    Sometimes a learner is reenrolled in a course automatically after completing the course. Check your completed enrollments to ensure you have received credit for completing the course.

  • I completed a lesson, but it did not register as complete.

    First, ensure you met the score requirement. Even if you complete the lesson, you must also pass the tests.

    It's possible you did not close the lesson properly. Use the "Exit Course" button within the lesson to ensure the quiz results save properly.

    You may have clicked the "Complete Survey" button (at the end of the lesson), been redirected to a separate page, and then closed the browser from there without going back to the lesson and clicking the "Exit Course" button. This must be done for the system to save results.

  • My quiz has no submit button.

    Sometimes the submit button can be covered by the Windows task bar. To solve this issue, either unlock your Windows task bar or maximize their browser window.

  • Maximizing Window

    Check that your screen in maximized. In the top right corner, click the square to ensure the window fits on the screen. The next button is at the very bottom right of the lesson window as shown in the screenshots.

    1. Click resume, then click on the square the green arrow is pointing to

    2. Notice the square sows 2 squares, meaning the screen has been maximized.
    3. The Next and Previous buttons, along with the timeline show up at the bottom right of the screen.

    4. When done taking a course, it is extremely important NOT to close the processing window before it has completed recording your progress. Doing so can cause unforeseen issues, including not properly recording your progress.

    • Admin Questions

  • How do I assign someone to be an Admin?

    Sign in to your admin account. Click the dropdown menu from the Admin tab. Select Manage User Permissions. Select the user(s) to change in the left box, and click the > (right carat) button to move the selected user(s) to the right box. Click Next.

    Near the top of the page, under Administrator Permissions, click the Group Only (Group-Level) hotlink. This activates the checkboxes for all default group admin permissions; edit these permissions as you wish. Finally, click the Finish button at the bottom of the page.

  • Admin Overview

    Every company that buys from SnapAV has a group admin. By default, the admin has extra abilities that normal students do not have.

  • Enable/Disable a User

    1. Click on the Admin Tab, then select Enable/Disable a User.

    2. If deactivating a user, click Active next to the Include category, then enter the user’s name in the search field and click Search. If activating a user, click Disabled next to the Include category, then enter the user’s name in the search field and click Search. Select the user

    3. Click the arrow to move the user to the Change Active/Disabled Status List, then click Finish to complete the wizard.

    • Browser/Flash Compatibility

    Google Chrome

  • How Do I Update Chrome?

    Always use the latest browser software when interacting with the Snap University. Follow these steps to ensure that you're using the latest version of Google Chrome:

    1. Open the Google Chrome browser.
    2. In Google Chrome, click the vertical-dot icon in the top right of your screen
    3. Within the dropdown menu, hover over Help and then click About Google Chrome.
    4. A picture pops up. If you are already using the latest version of Google Chrome, you'll see a blue circle with a white check mark. If Chrome needs an update, it starts automatically. Once the update is done, you'll see the blue circle.

  • Chrome Flash Settings

    Courses within Snap University cannot display Flash content unless you add the Snap University URL to the list of sites approved to run Flash. To do this, follow these instructions:

    1. In the address bar, type chrome://settings/content

    2. On the Content Settings screen, find the Flash Player listing. Click the gray arrow.

    3. On this new page, click Add, then enter http://SnapAV.tortal.net in the text box. Once the URL is added, close the Content Settings screen.

    • Microsoft Edge / Internet Explorer

  • Microsoft Edge Does Not Support eLearning

    Microsoft Edge, while fast and relatively secure, removes many advanced tools used by other browsers. Without these tools, Edge does not properly handle the interactivity that Snap University requires.

    You can launch lessons in Snap University and watch them, but your progress will not be recorded.

    To record your progress and get credit for completing courses, you must use Chrome, IE, FireFox, or Safari.

  • Update IE

    Always use the latest browser software when interacting with the Snap University. By default, Internet Explorer automatically updates whenever Windows Updates. If you turned off Windows Updates, visit https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/17621/internet-explorer-downloads to check for newer versions of IE.
